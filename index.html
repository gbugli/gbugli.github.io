    SBA Loan Approval

    Project developed as part of my undergraduate Big Data course. The goal of the analysis is to predict whether or not a loan should be approved based on archives provided by the U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA).     Australia Weather Analysis

    Analysis of weather data is traditionally performed using complex physical models that nonetheless provide fairly inaccurate predictions due to instability of the weather conditions.
